A Comprehensive Python-Based Accounting Solution

Executive Summary

This document outlines the design of a comprehensive, modular accounting software implemented in Python, specifically tailored to comply with Georgian legislation and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). The software aims to provide Georgian businesses with a reliable, compliant, and efficient solution for managing their financial operations.

Based on extensive research of Georgian tax regulations, IFRS requirements, and local business practices, this solution incorporates all necessary components for full legal compliance while maintaining international accounting standards.

Georgian Legislation Compliance

  • VAT rate of 18% with automatic calculation
  • Support for 100,000 GEL VAT registration threshold
  • Pension system with 2+2+2 contribution scheme
  • Monthly VAT returns due by the 15th
  • Georgian Lari (GEL) as primary currency
  • Support for Georgian data privacy laws

IFRS Compliance

  • Full IFRS Standards implementation (Blue Book 2023)
  • Support for IFRS for SMEs where applicable
  • Double-entry bookkeeping system
  • Multi-currency transaction handling
  • Comprehensive financial statement generation
  • Audit trail for all transactions
